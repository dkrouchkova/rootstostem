Our Mission

Roots to STEM is a web based application aimed at empowering and encouraging young women to pursue careers in the the fields of science, technology, engineering and mathematices.

By connecting girls and young women to mentors, namely other women who are working in a STEM career, as well as providing other resources, we hope to decrease the disparity between men and women in the STEM field.
